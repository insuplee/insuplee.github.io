Insup Lee

I am serving as a captain in the army at the Agency for Defence Development (ADD). I am also a Ph.D. student at the Department of Information Security, Korea University under the supervision of Prof. Wonjun Lee in NetLab. My main research interests include Deep Learning, Wireless Communications, and Cybersecurity. Prior to this, I received B.S. in cyber defense from Korea University in 2018.
