Insup Lee
Researcher @ Abu Dhabi, UAE
Ph.D. Candidate @ Korea University

I am an AI & Security Researcher based in Abu Dhabi, UAE, working on generative models for cybersecurity and drones. Previously, I spent five years as a researcher at the Agency for Defense Development (ADD), conducting research in AI-driven cybersecurity.

I am also a Ph.D. candidate in Cybersecurity at Korea University, where I earned my B.E. in Cyber Defense (CYDF).

My research interests lie at the intersection of AI and cybersecurity, focusing on generative models, AI-driven security, network security, and secure communications
