Insup Lee

Hi! I am a captain of ROK Army, currently working in Abu Dhabi, UAE. Prior to this, I completed five years of AI-based cybersecurity research at the Agency for Defense Development (ADD). I am in my last year of a seven-year mandatory military service, which will conclude in May 2025.

I am also a Ph.D. candidate in cybersecurity at Korea University, advised by Prof. Wonjun Lee (Network and Security Research Lab.). Prior to that, I received my B.Eng. in Cyber Defense (CYDF) from Korea University.

My primary research interest lies at the intersection of AI and network/cybersecurity, especially addressing diverse issues with generative models.
