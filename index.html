Insup Lee
Army Captain & Ph.D. Student

Hi! I am an army captain working at the Agency for Defense Development (ADD). After receiving my B.Eng. degree in Cyber Defense (CYDF), Korea University, I am entering the sixth year of my seven-year mandatory military service. I am also a Ph.D. student in Cybersecurity at Korea University, under the supervision of Prof. Wonjun Lee in Network and Security Research Lab. (NetLab).

My research area generally covers network and AI security, specifically adversarial machine learning, generative models, and AI-based cybersecurity. I believe that this research will contribute to next-generation security.
