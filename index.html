Insup Lee
Army Captain & Ph.D. Student

Hi! I am an army captain working at the R.O.K. Cyber Operations Command, after finishing five years of AI-based cybersecurity research in the Agency for Defense Development (ADD).

I am also a fifth-year Ph.D. student in cybersecurity at Korea University, advised by Prof. Wonjun Lee in Network and Security Research Lab. Prior to that, I received my B.Eng. in Cyber Defense (CYDF) from Korea University in 2018.

My primary research interest is at the Intersection of AI and Network/Cybersecurity, especially addressing data insufficiency issues with Generative Models.
